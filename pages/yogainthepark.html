Enjoy a relaxing, multi-level yoga session at the park, Miami. Concentrate on the view of the turquoise ocean, listen to the sound of the waves, and let yourself be guided personally by the instructor into a state of relaxation. These sessions are meant for both beginners and advanced practitioners, and establish level variations and tiers. Finally, leave with some food for thought, with a wisdom quote from the yogi. If you love yoga, this is your chance for an exciting day out!
